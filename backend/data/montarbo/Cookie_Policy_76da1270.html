Foreword
AEB uses cookies on the Site to enhance your experience with our site and web contents. Cookies are small text files that the Site sends to your terminal. Your terminal stores them and sends them back to the Site during the same session or upon your next visit. AEB also uses so-called third-party cookies, which are managed by websites other than the Site for the purposes set out below. Cookies may be retained only as long as you use the Site (i.e. session cookies), in order to make your browsing safer and more efficient, or for a longer period of time regardless of session duration (i.e. persistent cookies).

The cookies used by AEB enable us to determine which sections and contents of the Site attract more visitors or views, and help us adapt them to users’ preferences and improve our services among other things in order to, but not only, tailor our communication strategy to users and their interests.
In addition, AEB may use cookies to: i) authenticate you, provided that you registered to the Site to create a user account; ii) avoid fraudulent use of your user account; iii) identify perpetrators in the event cybercrimes are committed to the detriment of the Site or of third parties; iv) conduct market or statistical research of data in aggregate, anonymous form using the analytics cookies described below at paragraph Google Analytics.

Site Cookies
This Site uses the following types of cookies:

Essential cookies (necessary for browsing):
These cookies are strictly necessary to enable the Site to operate and be viewed correctly and make it possible to browse the Site using its essential features. These kinds of cookies do not gather information about you and that could be used for marketing purposes. Essential cookies are also used to store your data in the different pages during a browser session. Accepting essential cookies is a pre-requisite for using the Site correctly, and blocking them could prevent or strongly impair the ability to browse around the Site.

Google Analytics cookies:
AEB uses the web analytics service offered by Google, Inc. to monitor the statistical analysis of user traffic on the Site in aggregate, anonymous form in order to better understand how users use the Site and adapt it consequently in order to improve it. During your visit, AEB automatically gathers and stores the following information: (i) the domain and host from which you access the Internet; (ii) the address of the website from which you connected to the Site, if available; (iii) the date and time at which you connected to the Site, how long you remained connected and which pages were viewed; (iv) your Internet Protocol address (IP); (v) the operating system installed on your computer (or other media) and the browser you use; (vi) the demographics in anonymous form of users who access the www.dbtechnologies.com Site.

For further information on Google Analytics cookies, please visit the web page: https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout

The information gathered through Google Analytics cookies is such that it cannot be traced back to you by AEB on its own. Under no circumstances is information on your personal identity collected other than as specifically and expressly authorised by you.
