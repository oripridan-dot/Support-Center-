      Overview

      Complete Hardware/Software Recording Kit

      Everything You Need to Record and Produce at Home

      AudioBox 96 Studio ultimate is a complete recording and production bundle. Get everything you need to start recording guitar (or anything else) in a single purchase!

      The heart of the AudioBox 96 Studio is PreSonus' best-selling AudioBox USB® 96 interface, which captures your recordings in stellar studio quality. Its two front-mounted combo XLR and ¼" combo jacks let you connect a pair of mics for vocals, acoustic guitar or electric guitar cabinets. Alternatively, you can simply plug your electric guitars in directly and record direct, using Studio One's Ampire for world-class guitar amp and cab tones - and without your cranked Bassman angering your neighbors. And since there are two inputs, you and a friend can record guitar and bass or vocals and guitar simultaneously to separate tracks to mix later.

      The M7 large-diaphragm condenser microphone is the perfect mic for capturing acoustic guitar, but it doubles well on vocals, and the stand is included. The crystal-clear HD7 headphones will help you avoid any mic feedback issues during your recordings, let you closely monitor your performance, and also let you mix and produce late into the night without bothering anybody.

      Also included are PreSonus' best-selling Eris E3.5 monitor speakers. Unlike consumer-grade bookshelf speakers, monitors are tuned for a flat frequency response, meaning there's no over-hyped bass or under-represented midrange. When recording and producing music, it's important to hear an honest, accurate representation of what you're actually recording. The Eris E3.5s are as honest as speakers in their class can be, and you'll probably find yourself using them for regular ol' music listening as well.

      Finally, you get a perpetual license for Studio One Pro 7, so your productions will be a step ahead from day one.

      We even include all the cables required to hook it all up, so you'll hit the ground running right out of the box and get your creative vision out into the world faster than anyone else.

      Features

      AudioBox USB 96

      • PreSonus' all-time best-selling USB audio interface, now in sleek 25th-anniversary black
      • Record guitar and vocals at the same time: Two crystal-clear, class-A mic preamps with +48v provide power for condenser mics
      • Combo inputs let you record line-level sources like keyboards
      • Zero-latency hardware monitoring lets you hear yourself in real-time via internal analog mixer
      • High-volume headphone output lets you monitor your performance loudly and clearly while recording so you get a great take every time.
      • MIDI In/Out lets you connect synths, drum machines, and controllers
      • Balanced ¼" Main Outputs for connecting professional studio monitors
      • Professional Recording Software included: Studio One Pro License
      • USB cable included
      • USB bus-powered: no wall wart required!

      M7 Condenser microphone

      • Large-diaphragm design makes it perfect for capturing the nuance of acoustic guitar and voice
      • +48v phantom-powered via AudioBox USB 96
      • Mic stand adapter included
      • 9' / 3 m XLR cable included

      HD7 Headphones

      • High resolution neodymium drivers for optimal sound quality
      • Extended bass response so you'll hear the lowest lows
      • Built tough to withstand abuse
      • Lightweight and comfortable with long-lasting ear pads

      Eris E3.5 monitors

      • Get a pure and accurate representation of the music you make thanks to an honest frequency response with no enhanced or underrepresented frequencies
      • High- and low-frequency tuning controls let you tailor your sound to your environment
      • Front-panel headphone amplifier for listening in private
      • 50 watt (25W/side), of Class AB power for surprisingly high, clean volume
      • Connect nearly anything thanks to balanced ¼-inch TRS, unbalanced 1/8-inch stereo, and RCA inputs
