AudioBox® iTwo Studio

      Overview

      Get everything you need to produce professional podcasts and music alike with the iTwo Studio complete recording and production package in a single purchase! At the heart of the AudioBox iTwo Studio is the AudioBox iTwo interface, which has two combo jacks to capture your voice and instruments in stellar studio quality. It works with Windows, Mac, iPad, and iOS, so you can record very nearly anywhere. It's compatible with both microphones and real instruments, so it's the right tool for the job - wherever imagination takes you!

      The versatile M7 condenser microphone is also included, ideal for recording... well, everything; from podcasts to acoustic guitar to voice-over dialogue. The crystal-clear HD7 headphones will help you avoid any mic feedback issues during your live streams, while also letting you mix and produce late into the night without bothering anybody.

      When you're done recording to the free Capture Duo for iPad, you can wirelessly transmit your tracks to any computer on your Wi-Fi network running Studio One for mixing and postproduction.

      Finally, you get over $1000 USD of inspiring professional audio software - including Studio One Artist and the Studio Magic Suite, so your productions will be a step ahead from day one. We even included all the cables required to hook it all up, so you'll hit the ground running right out of the box and get your creative vision out into the world faster than anyone else.

      Highlights

      World-class recording software included.

      The AudioBox® iTwo Studio comes with PreSonus® Studio One™ Artist and the Studio Magic Software Suite for a complete recording solution. Studio One offers unlimited tracks and plug-ins and is easy to learn and use. Studio Magic includes over $1,000 USD of music software, including plug-ins, loops, virtual instruments, effects and tutorials.

      Capture® Duo recording software for iPad.

      Free PreSonus® Capture® Duo for iPad is a perfect companion for the AudioBox® iTwo, enabling you to record and play two stereo tracks simultaneously with up to 24-bit, 96 kHz fidelity. You can even record and save songs on an iPad, then wirelessly transfer them to Studio One™. Or record up to 32 tracks with affordable Capture for iPad!
