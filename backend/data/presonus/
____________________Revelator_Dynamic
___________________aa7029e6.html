      Overview

      The Revelator Dynamic USB microphone takes the clarity and warmth of our fan-favorite PD-70 microphone and adds easy-to-use, customizable presets to polish your voice further. Need to add backing tracks, Zoom calls, or gameplay audio? Revelator Dynamic has you covered with a built-in mixer, simplifying blending your voice with audio from other applications as well as creating the perfect monitor, stream, or podcast mix.

      The custom-built microphone capsule in the Revelator Dynamic is built to capture your voice cleanly and clearly while eliminating handling noise and breathiness that can get between you and your recording. Thanks to superior off-axis rejection, you can set up your home studio in just about any space - and unwanted reverberance, electrical hum, and the random car driving by will be left behind, leaving only the pristine, natural sound of your voice.

      Eight professionally crafted presets to make your voice sound polished right from the start. Like what you hear? Revelator Dynamic gives you the option to record your vocals fully processed, making for less work in post-production.

      Revelator Dynamic makes it easy to hear your performance loud and clear. The simple onboard Monitor control makes creating a great monitor mix fast and uncomplicated. Best of all, you can monitor through the Revelator Dynamic's amazing onboard processing so your vocals will sound polished, and you'll get the perfect take that much easier.

      Features

      • Professional bus-powered USB-C®-compatible dynamic microphone for vocal recording, podcasting, streaming, vlogging, gaming, and more
      • Custom-designed dynamic microphone capsule provides vocal clarity and superior off-axis rejection
      • Professionally crafted Presets make you sound like a pro with a single click. You can also create your own custom presets to share, save, or produce on the go.
      • Integrated mixer with two dedicated loopback audio channels to add backing tracks, Skype calls, gameplay audio, and more!
      • Effects processing lets you polish your sound with reverb, compression, EQ, and fantastic Voice FX - all built into Revelator Dynamic

      • PTS-1 extendable desktop mic stand included
      • Headphone output with volume control and zero-latency monitoring
      • Studio-quality sound: 96 kHz / 24-bit operation for pristine audio recording
      • Recording Software included: Studio One Artist v6 and Studio Magic software bundle including music tutorials, tons of sound samples and virtual instruments.

      Highlights

      Free Software Included - Studio One Pro 6 Artist Edition

      Your hardware registration includes Studio One 6, Artist Edition — a great way to start recording and a powerful entryway into the Studio One universe. Users can upgrade to Studio One Pro when they're ready to take their game to the next level. Compatible with virtually all macOS, and Windows.

      Analog classics under the hood.

      Revelator Dynamic provides State Space-modeled analog processing onboard with the same Fat Channel gate, compressor, and EQ our StudioLive Series III mixers offer. Like what you hear? All processing can be printed to your recording, or just used for your monitor mix, you decide.

      Ready to go live when you are.

      Revelator Dynamic’s presets are stored on the mic, so you can take your sound with you. Connect directly to your iPad Pro, or grab your Camera Connection Kit and connect your Revelator to your Lightning- or USB-C-equipped iPhone or iPad. Only Revelator brings studio-quality sound to Facebook Live, IGTV, or anywhere else you stream from your mobile device.
