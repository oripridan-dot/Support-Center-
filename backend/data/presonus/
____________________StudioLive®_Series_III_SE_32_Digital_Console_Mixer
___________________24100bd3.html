      Overview

      Designed to inspire and built to perform, StudioLive SE delivers pristine, ultra-low noise studio-quality sound to the stage in a full-sized one-to-one 32-channel frame. Dial in your mix with an arsenal of audio processors and effects, including meticulous digital models of coveted EQs, compressors, limiters, and reverb.

      But live sound for touring, installations, and houses of worship demands more than pristine audio and powerful effects. With the StudioLive SE 32, you get access to capabilities beyond any solution in its class—like seamless multitrack recording, playback and streaming with integrated Studio One Pro and Capture software, remote access mix, console management, and one-click virtual soundcheck.
      Expand your system and stay industry-ready—StudioLive SE connects effortlessly to networked StudioLive Stage Boxes and any Milan-compatible I/O, while its sleek design, intuitive layout, ergonomic feel, and high-contrast UI maximize comfort and streamline workflow during long sessions.

      Highlights

      Mix. Record. Stream. Control.

      StudioLive SE lets you manage your entire workflow—live mixing, multitrack recording, playback, and even streaming—without leaving the console. Integrated Capture® software enables one-click Virtual Soundcheck, while the 64x64 USB interface and onboard SD multitrack recorder offer seamless capture options. And everything works right out of the box with Studio One® Pro DAW.

      Remote Control from Anywhere

      With Metro™, our most advanced remote access suite ever, you can control routing, levels, processing—even complete configuration—via the internet from anywhere in the world. Use Universal Control locally on any tablet or mobile device, and let performers manage their own monitor mixes using QMix®-UC.

      Next-Level Audio Processing

      Powered by our dual-core FLEX DSP Engine, StudioLive SE includes studio-grade effects and signal processing—featuring all 8 Fat Channel compressor models and all 7 EQs from the Fat Channel Collection, now standard. Add expanded Digital Reverb XL presets, a variable limiter release, and intuitive FAT channel workflow—and you’ve got an analog-style experience with modern precision.
