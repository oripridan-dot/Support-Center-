Community Guidelines

These guidelines apply to all MyPreSonus users and Studio One Pro+ members wishing to view posts or participate in Studio One Pro+ Community Channels.

Guidelines for Community Channels:

• Treat others online as you would treat them in real life.
• Be tolerant towards other viewpoints; respectfully disagree when opinions do not align.
• Respect the privacy and personal information of other members.
• Communicate with courtesy and respect.

Please do not:

• Make personal attacks on other members.
• Use defamatory remarks or make false statements against others.
• Post prejudiced comments or profanity.
• Bully or make inflammatory remarks to other members.
• Post anything subject to copyright for which you do not have permission (see here).
