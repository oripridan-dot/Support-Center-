In this video, Dom Sigalas shows you how to create the reverse reverb effect in Cubase that works perfectly in any other DAW. Reverse reverb is one of the key sound effects for building momentum and excitement in the sounds of your music production. Dom looks at a fast way to create a reverse reverb vocal, carefully mixing it with the other elements of your production. By working with reverb effects in Cubase like this, you can create a real sense of drama in your song.
