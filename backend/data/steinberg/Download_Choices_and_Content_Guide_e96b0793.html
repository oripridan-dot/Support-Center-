Download Choices and Content Guide

The Steinberg Download Assistant (SDA) allows you to download and install all of Steinberg’s applications, expansions, and tools. By selecting your Cubase version (Pro, Artist, Elements, AI, or LE) on the left side of the SDA, you’ll see a list of available downloads on the right.

Note: Your license includes only the products listed under the ‘My Product Downloads’ category. Other product titles must be purchased or first licensed through a trial.

You can opt to install everything at once, or select individual downloads. This guide will help you navigate the list by explaining the different categories and individual titles.

In the Steinberg Download Assistant (SDA) for Cubase, you’ll find the Cubase application along with several content categories. The only required download is the Cubase application in the first row — this is the core program you’ll use to run Cubase. All other categories include optional plug-ins, sound content, and tools that are part of your Cubase license.

Virtual Instruments & Libraries

These downloads include instrument plug-ins and their preset libraries. Depending on your edition (Pro, Artist, Elements, AI, or LE), you’ll find HALion Sonic, Groove Agent, Retrologue, and specific instrument libraries such as Iconica Sketch — our orchestral library for HALion.

Loops & Samples

Cubase comes with more than 30 GB of loops and samples, covering a wide range of genres — from electronic music and hip hop to cinematic sounds and drum and bass.

Tools

This section includes additional utilities that can be used within Cubase, such as SpectraLayers Go and Yamaha’s vocal synthesis tool Omnivocal.

Demo Projects

Several demo projects are available for download. You can find more details about the projects and their producers here: https://www.steinberg.net/cubase/demo-projects/

Cubase 15 - Application

In the first row, you’ll find the Cubase application. The version is indicated by the name, such as Cubase 15.0.5. All Cubase editions—Pro, Artist, Elements, AI, and LE—share the same installer. Your license will determine which version is launched.

Read Me

The Read Me file, located just below the application installer, provides additional links and information on obtaining support, accessing the release notes, and registering your software.

VST Instruments, libraries and other tools

Based on the edition of Cubase, several VST instrument plug-ins, expansion and additional tools are included in your license.

HALion Sonic

HALion Sonic is a versatile software instrument that provides a broad selection of high-quality sounds for music production. It includes an intuitive interface, advanced synthesis capabilities, and a vast library covering everything from acoustic instruments to cutting-edge electronic sounds.

HALion Sonic Selection

HALion Sonic Selection is a curated collection of essential sounds from HALion Sonic. It features a selection of high-quality presets, making it a great starting point for musicians and producers looking for professional sounds without the full HALion Sonic package.

  • Requires HALion Sonic

Drum Machine Vol 01 - Content

Drum Machine Vol 01 is a collection of electronic drum sounds for the Drum Machine. It includes classic and modern drum kits designed for a variety of genres, from hip-hop to techno.

Drum Machine Vol 02 - Content

Drum Machine Vol 02 extends the Drum Machine library with 40 kits produced in various genres including  electronic, hip hop and drum and bass produced by Wavetick, RawTekk and The Beat Butcha.

Groove Agent SE

Groove Agent SE is a powerful virtual drum instrument included with Cubase. It offers realistic acoustic drums, electronic beats, and percussion sounds, making it an essential tool for drum programming.

Groove Agent SE Content

The Groove Agent SE Content package includes a wide range of drum kits, samples, and grooves designed to enhance your beat-making experience. It provides professionally recorded acoustic and electronic drum sounds to suit various music styles.

  • Requires Groove Agent

Production Grooves

Production Grooves feeds Groove Agent with over 400 drum loops and presets for all contemporary styles of music. Whether you need basic drum accompaniment for a songwriting session, epic electronica or serious rock beats, Production Grooves has the answer.

  • Requires Groove Agent

Retrologue

Retrologue is a virtual analog synthesizer that emulates classic analog synth sounds. It features three oscillators, multiple filter types, an extensive modulation matrix, and built-in effects, making it perfect for crafting rich, warm, and vintage tones.

Retrologue Content

The Retrologue Content package contains a collection of presets and sound libraries designed to showcase the capabilities of Retrologue. It includes classic analog leads, basses, pads, and experimental sounds.

  • Requires Retrologue

Padshop

Padshop is a granular synthesizer that allows users to create atmospheric, evolving, and experimental sounds. With its unique synthesis engine, it is perfect for ambient music, sound design, and cinematic compositions.

Padshop Content

The Padshop Content package includes a collection of high-quality presets and samples that demonstrate the full potential of Padshop’s granular synthesis. It provides lush textures, ambient soundscapes, and evolving pads.

  • Requires Padshop

Writing Room Synths

Writing Room Synths is a collection of production-ready, authentic vintage synths with rich leads, warm basses, and dreamypads, straight from the world’s best songwriting rooms, from LA to Stockholm.

  • Requires HALion Sonic

Iconica Sketch

Iconica Sketch is a compact orchestral library designed for quick and easy orchestration. It provides a range of high-quality instrument samples, including strings, brass, woodwinds, and percussion, making it ideal for cinematic and symphonic compositions.

  • Requires HALion Sonic

Verve

Verve is an expressive felt piano instrument with a warm, intimate, and organic sound. It captures the subtle nuances of a real piano, making it perfect for emotional film scoring, soft ballads, and delicate solo performances.

  • Requires HALion Sonic

Caleidoscope

A vibrant collection of experimental sounds, glitch textures, and evolving soundscapes designed for the presets of the Sampler Track.

EDM Toolbox

The EDM Toolbox comes with 30 new construction kits, ready to open up new creative avenues and fuel your next production. Construction kits are groups of 25 to 30 MIDI loops, routed to predefined instruments (including mix settings) to deliver immediate inspiration.

  • Requires HALion Sonic, Groove Agent, Retrologue and Padshop

Vintage Verb Collection

Additional presets with dedicated impulse responses for REVerence, the premium reverb plug-in included in Cubase.

Rock Pop Toolbox

Allen Morgan Rock-Pop Toolbox includes 30 construction kits, each with 25 to 30 audio and MIDI loops for contemporary music production.

  • Requires HALion Sonic, Groove Agent, Retrologue and Padshop

SpectraLayers Go

This compact version of the acclaimed visual audio editing tool allows you to visualize and accurately clean up your audio. Thanks to the state-of-the-art source separation engine, you can separate the vocal part from any kinds of audio tracks, whether you want to remix your favorite song or create mash-ups.

Yamaha Omnivocal

Experience natural vocals that push the limits of vocal synthesis. The simple interface and seamless workflow make creating expressive results effortless and inspiring every time.

Sample Packs

Cubase includes a variety of high-quality audio sample packs of different genres, each containing between 300 and 1,000 loops and one-shot samples for your productions. All loops are tagged with BPM information, ensuring they automatically match your project tempo when played through the MediaBay. Additionally, melodic samples include tonal information, allowing for easy alignment with your project’s root note. The samples can be used within your project, in the Sampler Track, Drum Machine or Groove Agent.
