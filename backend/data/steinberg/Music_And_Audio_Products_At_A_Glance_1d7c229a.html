Music And Audio Products At A Glance

Steinberg has a tradition of pioneering new technologies, upon which its entire product range is based.

Offering studio-grade software and hardware for music and audio, the company's comprehensive range of tools is meticulously tailored to the needs of discerning composers and musicians, producers, sound designers and other audio creatives.

Steinberg's product portfolio is ideal for recording and mixing sound, music production, podcasting, post-production, mastering and scoring for all kinds of audio projects, from the smallest to the biggest and most complex.
