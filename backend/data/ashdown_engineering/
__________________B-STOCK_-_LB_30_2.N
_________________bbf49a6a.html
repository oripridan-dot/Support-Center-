Unlock a new realm of bass tone with the Ashdown LB 30 2.N. Bass Amplifier. This 30-Watt marvel beautifully blends the timeless warmth of valve amps with the precision of modern dynamic response technology, making it a must-have for today's bassists.
 
At its core, the LB 30 2.N. features an EL84-equipped power section, delivering a harmonious balance of power and portability. Ideal for small gigs, it won't overwhelm your audience with volume, and its ergonomic design ensures easy transportation. The preamp stage utilizes ECC83 and ECC82 tubes, offering both Active and Passive gain inputs, providing versatility to accommodate various bass guitars.
 
What sets this amplifier apart is the integration of Two notes Torpedo technology. Built on convolution techniques, it begins with precise measurements of actual cabinets and microphone setups, capturing a diverse range of tones, from the iconic ABM series to the specialized CL-310 cab designed for Guy Pratt. With six emulated cabinets to choose from (including options with horns/tweeters), you have access to ten distinct cabinet sounds via a user-friendly 6-way rotary knob.
 
The LB 30 2.N. is equipped with versatile inputs and outputs to cater to your every need. USB connectivity allows you to edit and update on-board IR cabinets, and the USB CHARGE socket conveniently charges your devices. A direct balanced Torpedo IR output ensures seamless integration with your studio interface or front-of-house system. MIDI in & Out ports enable control of your amp IR's and effects using a compatible MIDI foot-switch. With options for 4 and 8-ohm speakers, a direct DI output, a headphone out, and the Torpedo line in XLR, this amplifier covers all your connectivity requirements.
