OriginAL C210T-300 Kickback Combo

£599.00

Cut the weight not the tone.

Delivering 300 watts of pure Ashdown bass tone via two 10" speakers whilst weighing in at just 14.5kg(32lbs) the OriginAL C210-300 is the perfect antidote to heavy and cumbersome bass equipment of years gone by.

A comprehensive 5 band EQ means it is easy to dial in your exact tone and the front-facing FX loop and XLR DI output are more tips of the hat to modern convenience. A headphone out is also incorporated for silent late night sessions.

Beyond that, its kickback design allows the combo to be used either as a floor monitor or in standard vertical position. Tough carpet covering and a steel grill complete the package. 

With ample power, lightweight construction, great sound, and player-friendly features, the OriginAL C210T is well worth your attention.
