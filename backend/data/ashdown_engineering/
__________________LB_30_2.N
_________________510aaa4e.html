30 Watts of Vintage Power with Dynamic Reponse

Classic Valve Warmth Meets Modern Tech

The Ashdown LB 30 2.N is a 30-watt all-valve bass amplifier that seamlessly blends the organic warmth of EL84-driven valve tone with modern precision. Designed for today's bassists, it offers the perfect balance of power, portability, and tone, making it equally at home in the studio or on small stages.

Cab Sim Power at Your Fingertips

Two Notes Torpedo Built-In

What sets the LB 30 2.N apart is its onboard Two Notes Torpedo cabinet simulation technology. Using convolution-based IRs, it captures the sound of legendary Ashdown cabinets, from the iconic ABM range to the Guy Pratt CL-310. With a 6-way rotary knob, you can select from six cabinet emulations (with or without horns/tweeters), giving you access to ten distinct cab sounds instantly.

For Every Bass and Playing Style

Flexible Inputs and Tone Shaping

The preamp stage is powered by ECC83 and ECC82 tubes, with Active and Passive inputs to suit all basses. Whether you want fat cleans or valve-driven grit, the LB 30 2.N responds with versatility. Ergonomically designed and weighing just 10kg, it delivers portability without sacrificing sonic authority.

Plug in Anywhere, Record Everywhere

Full Connectivity for Stage and Studio

Packed with modern I/O, the LB 30 2.N is ready for any environment. A direct balanced Torpedo IR output integrates seamlessly with FOH systems or recording interfaces, while USB connectivity allows you to edit and update IR cabinets or charge devices via the USB CHARGE port. MIDI In/Out expands control, and the Torpedo Line In XLR even lets you re-amp recordings back into the cabinet sim. Add 4/8 Ohm speaker outputs, DI, FX loop, and a headphone out, and you've got complete flexibility.
